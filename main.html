My Favorite Holiday Destination: Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. It consists of over 1,000 coral islands, known for their crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Snorkeling at Banana Reef: Explore the underwater world and swim alongside colorful fish and coral formations.
  2. Visit Bioluminescent Beaches: Witness the magical glow of plankton in the water at night.
  3. Relax in Overwater Bungalows: Stay in luxurious bungalows perched above the turquoise lagoon.
  4. Explore Male, the Capital City: Discover local markets, historical sites, and vibrant street art.
  5. Dolphin Watching: Take a boat tour to spot playful dolphins in their natural habitat.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with shredded tuna, coconut, and spices.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish marinated in chili and garlic.
  • Bis Keemiya: Savory pastries filled with spiced fish or vegetables.
  • Rihaakuru: A fish-based thick paste served with rice.
  • Coconut Water: Fresh coconut water straight from the source!
